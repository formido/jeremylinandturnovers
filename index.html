The Myth of Jeremy Lin's Turnover Problem

Contrary to recent hype, Jeremy Lin does not have a turnover problem. A concise way to demonstrate this is to make a graph with each game's turnovers for the 13 games before Lin started playing major minutes and the 13 games since.


SOURCE: Basketball-Reference.com

It looks like the Knick's total team turnovers have stayed pretty much the same, doesn't it? In fact, the team has turned the ball over somewhat fewer times since Jeremy Lin took over.
